Introduction

Pizza is my favorite food because of its delicious taste, variety of toppings, and the way it brings people together.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables

Preparation

  1. Prepare the dough and let it rise.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven until golden and crispy.
